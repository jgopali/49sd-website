What are CTFs?

A CTF or (Capture the Flag) is a type of computer security competition in which the individual or team is given a wide range of challenges to solve. In order for the individual or team to be rewarded points they must submit the correct flag or answer, which is found by solving the challenges. We have created a mini CTF for you to try out. If you cant seem to find the flag use the hint button to give you a hand.
